FEATURES THAT HIT DIFFERENT

🏆

WEIGHT + REP PRs

We track both. Hit the same weight but knocked out 3 more reps? That's a PR. Progress doesn't always mean heavier — it means better.

After every session, we tell you exactly what to aim for next time. Specific targets for weight and reps — so you always know what you're chasing.
